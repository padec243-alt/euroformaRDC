# **App Name**: Euroforma RDC Digital

## Core Features:

- Course Listings: Display all available courses with details, schedules, and instructor information.
- Online Registration: Enable users to register for courses directly through the website.
- Interactive Gallery: Showcase photos and videos of past training sessions and events.
- News and Updates: Publish blog posts and articles related to industry trends, training tips, and company news.
- Contact Form: Provide a simple contact form for inquiries and support requests.
- Alumni Testimonials: Showcase success stories from past students.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark teal (#008080), echoing Euroforma RDC's logo for stability and professionalism.
- Background color: Light teal (#E0F8F8), a desaturated version of the primary, creating a calm backdrop.
- Accent color: Vibrant red (#FF3030), derived from one of the logo's accent colors, to draw the eye.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' sans-serif for a modern and impactful look.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif paired with 'Poppins' for longer text to ensure readability while maintaining a modern feel.
- Use modern, clean icons to represent different training categories and sections of the website.
- Implement a clean and modern layout with a clear navigation bar including: Accueil, À Propos, Formations, Gallerie, Actualités, Contact, and INSCRIVEZ-VOUS.
